Exploring Creativity

This is a great opportunity to experiment and think outside the box. Use materials you haven't, try new mediums, explore new styles. Have fun with your art. Play with different levels of meaning. Try different techniques like pop-ups or dried flowers. Play around with different styles like expressionism, pointillism, cross-hatching, or abstraction. Use this as a way of trying new things and finding new techniques you like and dislike from the process.

Expressing Emotion

This format is a fantastic place to vent about what you are feeling or going through in an expressive way. You can incorporate writing into your sketchbook to make more of an art journal style of book. Art is such a powerful way of expressing emotions so it a good source of inspiration for your work.
